A Poem Just For You

💖
In your eyes, I see my world complete, Loud and proud is your laughter that wakes the new day, And shawl of melodies, I float through your golden rays. Each and every day brings a gentle gaze so calming, And peace beyond the stormiest of worries I can now calm. Your splendor paints the air with every soft breath you take, Blushing rose, softest lavender blended with the dawn’s light. Your whispers rare, strange and tender with words so warm, And moments so simple turn into nights form. Your heart is so steady, a withstanding flame that hails, Drawing breath from the brightest of the stars in the sky. While cradling me, an embrace so soothing that tells This is the depth of love that enables my restless soul to fly. Vanishing is my boundless care, and forever ends where it begins, Measuring is the sky’s unending span, and so too the ocean’s depths. So in turn, I am yours, so devoted from the start, As I humble in thanks that your heart persists. So through every twist and every turn with joyous strain, And in my arms, entwine your fingers and bend. As I cherish your radiance, each and every single gain, A story boundless, that each page transcends.

A Letter From My Heart

💌
With All My Love

My Dearest Love,

In the instant our lives collided, my heart familiarized itself with the rare bond we share. Your essence, both the warmth and beauty, illuminates my life, treating me with the utmost affection. It feels as if the universe sighs and whispers your name into my ear, leading me towards the best gift any human can hope for; your love. Waking up every day brings me a sense of gratitude and thankfulness, especially for the serene sound of your laughter. It’s a melody that cherishes my heart that rises my world even higher than the sun’s first rays. Your smile illuminates my life with hope, transforming me into a better person which helps me pull through even the hardest days. Your generosity and steadfastness shapes me into someone better. With the essence of my being, I can say that your patience and understanding is a form of salvation in a world in which people can not control their feelings. You have shown me what a true companion can be, as you have told me that love, in its essence, does not tackling the world together, but rather sighing with a soft lovely word and a gaze intertwined.As I reminisce about our moments together—the laughter, the dreams, the secrets we’ve shared—I’m engulfed with gratitude. You have been my greatest adventure and you continue to be my greatest adventure. Confidante, my sanctuary, you embrace me and I find excitement and peace, a beautiful rare harmony. I have made the promise to myself to cherish you every day. I promise to listen to every word you say. I promise to root for you, to treat your dreams as my own and to love you fiercely through life’s many ways. I promise to move heaven and earth to demonstrate the many ways I’m devoted to you. I can’t thank you enough for your laughter which lights up my day. I can’t thank you enough for the love and strength you give me as it steadies my spirit. I’m grateful every day that fate directed me to you.

Forever yours,
Your Devoted Heart
